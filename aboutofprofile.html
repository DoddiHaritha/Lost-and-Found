Lost and Found Portal

Reuniting People with Their Lost Belongings

About Us

The Lost and Found Portal is dedicated to helping individuals and organizations reconnect with lost items. We provide an easy-to-use platform to post, search, and recover belongings.

Features

  • User Registration: Secure login for individuals and organizations.
  • Listing System: Create posts for lost or found items with details and photos.
  • Search & Filters: Advanced search tools and map-based location tracking.
  • AI Matching: Intelligent suggestions for matching lost and found items.
  • Notifications: Alerts for updates and potential matches.
  • Mobile App: Stay connected on the go with our app.

Target Audience

Our platform serves individuals, pet owners, travelers, and organizations such as airports, malls, and event venues.

Values

  • Trust: Providing a secure platform.
  • Community: Encouraging collaboration and kindness.
  • Efficiency: Leveraging technology for quick recoveries.
